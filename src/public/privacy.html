Privacy Policy

At Screenshoted, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • URLs of websites you want to capture
  • Browser and device information
  • Usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide screenshot services
  • To improve our service
  • To maintain service security
  • To communicate with you if needed

Data Storage

Screenshots are temporarily stored and automatically deleted after processing. We do not permanently store any screenshots or personal information.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our service. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at: admin@screenshoted.com
